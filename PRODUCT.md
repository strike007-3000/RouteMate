# RouteMate Product Context

## Register
Product. Design serves the task (travel itinerary planning, routing, logistics).

## Users
Travelers and coordinators who need high-density, legible, and real-time transit and weather data on mobile or desktop devices.

## Voice
Sleek, premium, dark-mode first, reassuring, and highly accurate.

## Anti-references
- Plain, generic browser colors (plain red, plain blue, plain green).
- Sharp, boxy corners (must use `rounded-[24px]` and `rounded-[32px]`).
- Hype-based purple/pink gradients or generic SaaS templates.
- Overcomplicated, multi-colored buttons (must stick to primary action style).
